We are pickleball pioneers.


MPC is the first pickleball club in the US by college students, for college students.

It is our mission to unite diverse communities across the University of Minnesota to learn and enjoy one of America's fastest growing sports. Whether you want to become World #1 or just meet a few new people...we're glad you're here.


During the regular academic year, we meet every Friday from 4-6p in the RecWell North Gym.
